Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Registration
How do I open a new account?
You can open a new account by visiting our nearest branch with valid ID and proof of address, or through our online banking platform.
What documents do I need for registration?
You'll need a valid government-issued ID, proof of address, and tax identification number.
Online Banking
How do I reset my online banking password?
You can reset your password through the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page or by contacting our customer service.
Is online banking secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your online banking activities.
Products & Services
What types of loans do you offer?
We offer various types of loans including personal loans, home loans, business loans, and vehicle loans.
How do I apply for a credit card?
You can apply for a credit card through our website, mobile app, or by visiting any of our branches.
